Ping Pong Game

How to Play:
  • Click where you want to throw the ball. The ball will be launched toward that point.
  • Try to land the ball in all the cups arranged in a pyramid. The number of cups and their spacing increases with difficulty.
  • A fan (wind) will push your ball left or right each throw. Watch the wind indicator and fan animation!
  • A moving bomb obstacle travels horizontally near the bottom. If your ball touches the bomb, the game will restart!
  • Collect power-ups for special effects!
  • Unlock achievements for special feats!
  • Try Timed Mode for an extra challenge!
  • Enjoy animated cups when you score!
Good luck and have fun!
