Beer Pong Game

How to Play:
  • Move your mouse to set the aim direction (an arrow will show the direction).
  • Hold the spacebar to pull back the lever and increase shot power.
  • Release the spacebar to shoot the ball!
  • Try to land the ball in all the red cups arranged in a pyramid.
  • A fan (wind) will push your ball left or right each throw. Watch the wind indicator!
  • Enjoy simple beer pong fun!
Good luck and have fun!
